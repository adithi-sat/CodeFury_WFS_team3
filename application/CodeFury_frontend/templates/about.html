Features of the Bug Tracking System

Role: Project Manager

As a Project Manager, you have the following capabilities:

  • Oversee all active projects and monitor their progress.
  • Assign tasks to developers and testers based on project requirements.
  • Set project deadlines and track team performance.
  • Generate and view comprehensive project reports.
  • Manage team members and their roles within the project.

Role: Developer

As a Developer, your responsibilities include:

  • View and update tasks assigned by the Project Manager.
  • Submit bug fixes and update the status of the bug accordingly.
  • Collaborate with other developers and testers to ensure smooth project progress.
  • Access detailed bug reports to understand the issues.
  • Track the progress of bugs you've worked on.

Role: Tester

As a Tester, your duties include:

  • Report bugs and issues discovered during testing.
  • Reproduce bugs and verify bug fixes submitted by developers.
  • Work closely with developers to clarify bug details and expectations.
  • Prioritize bugs based on their severity and impact on the project.
  • Generate and view test case reports to ensure coverage.

Additional Features

  • Real-Time Notifications: Get instant updates on project changes, bug assignments, and status updates.
  • Advanced Search: Easily find bugs, tasks, and projects using filters and keywords.
  • Customizable Dashboard: Tailor your dashboard to display the information most relevant to your role.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Ensure that users only have access to the features and data relevant to their role.
  • Analytics and Reporting: Generate detailed reports on project performance, team productivity, and bug trends.
